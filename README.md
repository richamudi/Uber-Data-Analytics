# Uber-Data-Analytics

# 🚕 Uber Availability Analysis (Bangalore Airport Problem)

## 📌 Problem Statement
Users often face difficulty booking an Uber in Bangalore, especially for:
- City → Airport
- Airport → City  

This project analyzes the possible reasons behind this issue using data analytics.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To identify patterns and bottlenecks affecting Uber availability and provide insights that can help improve ride fulfillment.

---

## 📊 Approach

1. **Understanding the Problem**
   - When does the issue occur? (time of day)
   - Where does it occur? (pickup points)
   - Who is affected? (customers/drivers)

2. **Data Analysis using Python**
   - Libraries used:
     - NumPy
     - Pandas
     - Matplotlib / Seaborn

3. **Key Steps**
   - Data Cleaning
   - Handling missing values
   - Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
   - Identifying peak demand vs supply gaps

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

- 🚗 High demand during peak hours leads to ride shortages
- 📍 Airport routes show higher cancellation/no-car availability
- ⏰ Time-based patterns significantly impact booking success
- ❌ Driver unavailability is a major factor

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

##🚕 Airport Waiting Incentives

Encourage drivers to wait at airport zones by offering bonuses for idle time.
This helps ensure supply is readily available for incoming demand.

##⏳ Shift Premiums

Provide additional earnings or incentives to drivers who:

Complete airport trips
Stay active during high-demand airport hours

This improves driver participation during critical time windows.

##📍 Pre-positioning Fleet

Strategically position drivers in high-demand areas before peak hours.
Using demand forecasting, this reduces wait time and increases booking success rates.

##🔍 Wider Airport Search Radius

Expand the driver search radius for airport pickups.
This ensures:

Riders have higher chances of finding a cab
Drivers get more ride opportunities

##🎯 Key Impact

These strategies aim to:

Balance supply-demand gaps
Reduce ride cancellations
Improve customer experience
Increase driver earnings
